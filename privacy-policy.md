## 2. What data we collect

- **Account data** — e-mail address and display name (for authentication and Family
  sharing), and an authentication session.
- **Pet & Content data** — cat profiles (name, weight, age, breed, microchip, insurance
  details), notes, health/care records, reminders, expenses, and files you upload
  (photos, PDF documents). *Note: data about an animal is not, by itself, "personal data"
  under the GDPR, but uploaded documents may contain your name or address, so we protect
  them to the same standard as personal data.*
- **Family data** — the members of your Family group and their roles, so shared Content can
  be synchronised.
- **Subscription data** — your purchase and subscription status (processed by the app
  stores and RevenueCat; we do not receive your card details).
- **Usage & analytics data** — screens viewed and key in-app actions, app version,
  device type and language, associated with a pseudonymous identifier — **only if you have
  enabled analytics** (see §6).
- **Advertising data** — for free-tier users, an advertising identifier (Apple IDFA /
  Google Advertising ID) and limited ad-interaction data used to serve and measure ads,
  processed **subject to your consent** (see §6).
- **Technical data** — IP address and basic device information processed by our
  infrastructure providers to deliver and secure the service; crash/error reports **if and
  when crash reporting is enabled**.

We do not intentionally collect special-category data.

## 5. Family sharing — shared visibility

Content you add to a Family group is **visible to, and may be edited or deleted by, other
members of that group** according to their role. You control who you invite. Each member is
responsible for the Content they add. If you leave or are removed from a Family group, you
lose access to that group's shared Content.

## 6. Analytics and advertising

- **Analytics is off unless you consent.** We use **PostHog** (product analytics —
  screen views, funnels, key actions) hosted in the **EU**. You can turn analytics on or off
  at any time in **Settings** in the App; turning it off stops further collection.
- **Advertising (free tier only):** we show ads to free-tier users via **Google AdMob**.
  PRO removes all ads. Personalised ads and any use of your advertising identifier require
  your **consent**, which we obtain through the platform consent mechanisms — Google's
  consent management platform (UMP / IAB TCF) in the EEA and Apple's **App Tracking
  Transparency** prompt on iOS. If you decline, you may still see non-personalised
  (contextual) ads. You can change your choice in your device settings at any time.
- **Crash reporting (planned):** if enabled, crash/error diagnostics would be processed to
  fix defects; this Policy will be updated accordingly before it goes live.

## 9. Data retention

- Account, pet and Content data are retained while your account is active.
- When you delete your account (see §10), it is **deactivated and scheduled for deletion**
  with a **180-day recovery window** during which you can restore it by signing back in.
  After the window, your account and data are **permanently erased**; residual copies in
  encrypted backups are then removed within up to 30 days. If you want immediate erasure
  without the recovery window, contact us and we will honour your request.
- Analytics data is retained for up to 12 months and is pseudonymous.
- We may retain limited data longer where required to comply with legal obligations
  (e.g. accounting/tax records related to purchases).

## 10. Your rights under the GDPR

You have the right to:

- **access** a copy of your data (the App also provides a self-service **data export**);
- **rectify** inaccurate data (you can edit most data directly in the App);
- **erasure ("right to be forgotten")** — delete your account directly in the App under
  **Settings → Account → Delete account**. Deletion deactivates the account and schedules
  it for permanent erasure after a **180-day recovery window** (you can restore it by
  signing in during that time); after the window, all associated data — including pet data
  and uploaded files — is permanently removed from our servers (subject to the backup
  period in §9). You may request **immediate erasure** by contacting us;
- **restrict** or **object to** processing;
- **data portability** (receive your data in a machine-readable format — see the export
  feature);
- **withdraw consent** at any time for analytics/advertising, via Settings;
- **lodge a complaint** with a supervisory authority — in Estonia, the **Data Protection
  Inspectorate (Andmekaitse Inspektsioon)**, <https://www.aki.ee/>.

To exercise your rights, contact demetr35@gmail.com. We respond within one month, as
required by the GDPR.
